# Zen Browser - A New Era of Browsing

Zen Browser is an open-source browser built on Firefox that aims to deliver a **fast, distraction-free** experience without compromising your privacy. It positions itself as a calmer alternative in a market dominated by data-hungry browsers like Chrome.

By leveraging the Firefox engine and stripping away unnecessary bloat, Zen helps you stay productive with features like Workspaces and split view, while keeping trackers and telemetry out of your way.

---

## Key Features

* **Workspaces**
  Organize browsing into separate task-based tab groups. Each workspace lives in the sidebar and can run in isolated Firefox containers, making account switching and context separation painless.

* **Compact Mode**
  Hide toolbars and UI elements for a clean, immersive full-screen experience. Ideal for reading, watching content, or deep focus sessions.

* **Glance**
  Preview links in an overlay without leaving your current tab. Use Alt + Click to peek at content instantly.

* **Split View**
  Tile multiple websites side-by-side in the same window. Perfect for research, coding with docs open, or multitasking workflows.

## Under the Hood

Zen is built directly on Firefox, inheriting its security model, rendering engine, and rapid update cycle. New Firefox releases are usually merged within days.

Builds use **Profile-Guided Optimization (PGO)** and **Link-Time Optimization (LTO)** to squeeze out extra performance without sacrificing stability.

Privacy defaults are aggressive but sane:

* HTTPS-Only mode enabled
* Tracking protection on by default
* OCSP and phishing protection active
* No telemetry sent to Mozilla or Zen

All changes are open-source and configurable through internal settings and files.

Zen is still in **beta**, so occasional bugs are expected. Updates are frequent and the roadmap is public.
